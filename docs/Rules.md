## Constitution
- Increase Max Health by 5 points

# Stats
## Dodge Chance
The chance of dodging an attack (getting out of the way).
## Damage Protection
On a hit, how much of the damage is reduced.
dmg = atk - (prot / 2)
If your protection is 10, and the hit is 20, you take 15 points of damage.
